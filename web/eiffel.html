Eiffel Tower in Paris

The most enthralling allurement in Paris is the Eiffel Tower; the quintessential monument which finds its place among the Seven Wonders of the World. A masterpiece of architectural excellence, the Eiffel Tower is a must-see attraction in Paris. The Eiffel Tower is synonymous with the identity and popularity of Paris, and is one of the most visited monuments in the world. Nicknamed as La dame de fer (the iron lady), the Eiffel Tower is truly magnificent and stands proudly at a height of 300 metres.
